Duel Monsters VR

Duel Monsters VR is a fan-made Yu-Gi-Oh! virtual reality game. The DMVR Discord server has over 11,000 members, with over 100 active users daily. The viral success of the game can be attributed to its popularity on Youtube and Facebook, with over 5 million combined views on release. The game also led to the creation of many Duel Monsters VR content channels. The game has been featured in online articles, such as Road to VR , VR Scout, and Gaming Historia.
